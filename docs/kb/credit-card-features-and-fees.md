## Credit Card Features

Our credit cards are accepted at millions of merchants worldwide through the Visa and Mastercard networks. Key features include:

- Revolving credit line
- Installment conversion for eligible purchases
- Reward points on qualified purchases
- Purchase protection and travel insurance (selected card variants)

## Fees and Charges

### Annual Fee

The annual membership fee varies by card type:
- Classic: PHP 1,500 per year
- Gold: PHP 2,500 per year
- Platinum: PHP 5,000 per year

The annual fee may be waived based on minimum annual spend requirements. Check the specific card terms for details.

### Interest Rate

The monthly interest rate on outstanding balances is 2% per month (24% per annum), applied to the unpaid balance after the payment due date.

### Minimum Payment

The minimum payment due each month is 2% of the total outstanding balance or PHP 500, whichever is higher.

### Late Payment Fee

A late payment fee of PHP 850 or 6% of the minimum amount due (whichever is higher) is charged when the minimum payment is not received by the due date.

### Cash Advance Fee

Cash advances carry a fee of 3% of the transaction amount or PHP 500, whichever is higher, plus interest from the transaction date.

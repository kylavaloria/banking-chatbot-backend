## Personal Loan Application Requirements

To apply for a personal loan, you must meet the following basic eligibility criteria and submit the required documents.

## Eligibility

- Filipino citizen or resident alien
- At least 21 years old at the time of application and not more than 65 years old at loan maturity
- Regular employee with at least 1 year of continuous employment, or self-employed with a business operating for at least 2 years

## Required Documents for Employed Applicants

- Completed loan application form
- Valid government-issued ID (primary and secondary)
- Latest payslips (1 to 3 months)
- Certificate of Employment with compensation details
- Latest Income Tax Return (ITR) or BIR Form 2316

## Required Documents for Self-Employed Applicants

- Completed loan application form
- Valid government-issued ID
- Business Registration Certificate (DTI or SEC)
- Latest 2 years audited financial statements
- Latest 6 months bank statements

## Processing Time

Loan applications are typically processed within 5 to 7 banking days after complete submission of documents. Approval is subject to credit evaluation.

## Loan Proceeds

Upon approval, loan proceeds are credited directly to your bank account with us or issued as a manager's check, depending on the loan type.

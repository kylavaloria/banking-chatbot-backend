## How to Dispute a Billing Error or Incorrect Fee

If you believe you have been charged incorrectly — whether for a service fee, interest charge, or a transaction you did not authorize — you may file a dispute through any of the following channels:

### How to File a Dispute

1. **Customer service hotline** — Call our 24/7 hotline and request a billing dispute. Our representative will log the dispute and provide a reference number.
2. **Branch visit** — Visit any branch and complete a billing dispute form. Bring a valid ID and any relevant documents.
3. **Mobile banking app** — Navigate to the transaction in question and select "Dispute this transaction."

### What to Prepare

- The specific charge being disputed (date, amount, description)
- Any supporting documents such as receipts, screenshots, or correspondence with the merchant

### Investigation Timeline

Disputes are typically resolved within 10 to 15 banking days. Complex cases may take up to 30 days. You will be notified of the outcome through your registered contact details.

### Provisional Credit

For qualifying disputes, a provisional credit equal to the disputed amount may be applied to your account while the investigation is ongoing. This is subject to the bank's dispute policies.

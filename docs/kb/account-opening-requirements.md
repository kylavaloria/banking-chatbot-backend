## Requirements to Open a Bank Account

To open a personal savings or checking account, you will need to present the following at any of our branches:

## Primary Identification (any one)

- Valid government-issued ID with photo and signature
- Passport
- Driver's license
- National ID
- Postal ID
- SSS or GSIS ID
- Philippine National Police ID

## Secondary Identification (if primary ID lacks signature or photo)

- Any secondary government-issued ID
- Company or school ID with photo

## Additional Requirements

- Completed account opening form (available at the branch or downloadable from our website)
- Initial deposit (minimum amount varies by account type — see the Savings Account Fees document for details)
- Proof of billing or address if your ID does not show your current address

## For Joint Accounts

All account holders must be present at the time of opening and must each provide valid identification.

## For Minors

A parent or legal guardian must be present. The guardian must provide their own valid ID and sign the account opening form on behalf of the minor.

## Processing Time

Account opening is typically completed within 30 to 60 minutes at the branch, provided all documents are in order.

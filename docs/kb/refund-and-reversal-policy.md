## Refund and Reversal Policy

### Merchant Refunds

When a refund is initiated by a merchant for a card transaction, the credited amount typically appears on the account within 5 to 10 banking days from the date the merchant processes the refund. The actual timeline depends on the merchant's bank and payment network.

### Failed or Returned Transfers

If a transfer fails due to incorrect account details or the beneficiary bank returning the funds, the amount is credited back to the sender's account within 3 to 5 banking days.

### Disputing a Transaction

To dispute an erroneous charge or a transaction you did not authorize, contact our customer service within 60 days of the transaction date. You will need to provide:
- The transaction date and amount
- The merchant or beneficiary name
- Any reference or transaction number

Our team will investigate and respond within 10 banking days. During the investigation, a provisional credit may be applied to your account depending on the nature of the dispute.

### Non-Refundable Fees

Bank service charges, annual fees, and processing fees are generally non-refundable unless the charge was made in error by the bank.

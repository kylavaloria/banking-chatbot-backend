## Customer Support Channels

We offer multiple channels to assist you with your banking concerns.

## 24/7 Customer Service Hotline

Our customer service hotline is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including public holidays. You can reach us for:
- Account inquiries
- Card blocking and reporting
- Transaction disputes
- General banking concerns

## Mobile Banking Application

Our mobile banking app allows you to:
- Check balances and transaction history
- Transfer funds and pay bills
- Block or manage your card
- Submit inquiries and track status

## Email Support

For non-urgent inquiries, you may send an email to our official customer support address. Responses are provided within 1 to 2 banking days.

## Branch Visit

For concerns requiring document submission, identity verification, or in-person assistance, visit any of our branches during operating hours.

## Escalation Path

If your concern has not been resolved satisfactorily:
1. Request to speak with a branch manager or senior customer service officer
2. File a formal complaint through our official complaint channels
3. If still unresolved, you may file a complaint with the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) Consumer Protection Department

Response timelines for formal complaints: acknowledgment within 2 banking days, resolution within 15 banking days.

## How to Request Bank Certificates and Documents

We issue a range of official bank documents upon customer request. Common documents include bank certificates, account statements, and loan certificates.

## Types of Documents Available

- **Bank Certificate** — Certifies that you hold an account in good standing with our bank, including account balance and account type
- **Statement of Account (SOA)** — Detailed transaction history for a specified period
- **Certificate of Full Payment** — Issued upon full settlement of a loan
- **Loan Statement** — Current balance and payment history for an active loan

## How to Request

### At a Branch

1. Visit any branch with a valid ID
2. Complete the document request form
3. Pay the applicable fee (see fees below)
4. Indicate whether you want the document released on the same day or on a later date

### Through Mobile Banking

Selected documents such as account statements can be downloaded directly from the mobile banking application at no charge.

## Fees and Processing Times

- Bank Certificate: PHP 150 per copy, released within 1 to 2 banking days
- Account Statement (historical, beyond standard period): PHP 50 per month requested
- Certificate of Full Payment: PHP 100 per copy, released within 3 to 5 banking days

## Purpose Declaration

For bank certificates used for visa applications, loan applications at other institutions, or government requirements, indicate the purpose at the time of request so the certificate can include the appropriate wording.

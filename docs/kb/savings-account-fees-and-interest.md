## Savings Account Fees

### Minimum Initial Deposit

The minimum initial deposit to open a regular savings account is PHP 500. Premium savings accounts require a minimum initial deposit of PHP 5,000.

### Maintaining Balance

The minimum average daily balance (ADB) required to avoid a monthly fee is PHP 1,000 for a regular savings account. If the ADB falls below this amount, a monthly service fee of PHP 300 applies.

### ATM Card Fee

An annual ATM card fee of PHP 150 is charged upon issuance and on each anniversary date.

### Dormancy Fee

Accounts with no transactions for 24 consecutive months are classified as dormant. A dormancy fee of PHP 30 per month applies until the account is reactivated or the balance reaches zero.

## Interest Rates

Regular savings accounts earn interest at the prevailing rate set by the bank, currently at 0.10% per annum. Interest is credited to the account quarterly.

Premium savings accounts earn a higher interest rate of 0.25% per annum, credited monthly.

Interest rates are subject to change. The latest rates are posted at all branches and on our official website.

## Other Fees

- Account closure fee (within 30 days of opening): PHP 500
- Passbook replacement: PHP 150
- Statement of account (per request, beyond monthly statement): PHP 50 per month requested

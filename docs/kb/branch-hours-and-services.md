## Branch Hours and Services

Our branches are open Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM, and Saturday from 9:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Branches are closed on Sundays and all public holidays.

Extended banking hours (7:00 AM to 7:00 PM on weekdays) are available at selected main branches in central business districts. Check the branch locator on our website or mobile app for the nearest extended-hours branch.

## Services Available at Branches

All branches offer the following services:
- Account opening and document submission
- Cash deposits and withdrawals
- Cheque encashment and deposits
- Loan applications and document submission
- Card issuance and replacement
- Bank certificate and statement requests
- KYC update and identity verification

## ATM Services

Our ATM network is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. ATMs support cash withdrawals, balance inquiries, and fund transfers to accounts within the same bank.

## Contacting Your Branch

To reach your nearest branch, use the branch locator available on our official website or mobile banking application. You may also call our 24/7 customer service hotline for branch-specific inquiries.

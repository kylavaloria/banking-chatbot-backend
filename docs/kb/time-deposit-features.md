## Time Deposit Overview

A time deposit (also called a fixed deposit) is a savings product where you place a fixed amount for a specific period at a guaranteed interest rate. It is suitable for customers who do not need immediate access to their funds and want to earn a higher interest rate than a regular savings account.

## Minimum Placement

The minimum placement for a time deposit is PHP 10,000.

## Tenor Options

Time deposits are available in the following tenors:
- 30 days
- 60 days
- 90 days
- 180 days
- 360 days

## Interest Rates

Interest rates are higher for longer tenors. Indicative rates (subject to change):
- 30 days: 1.00% per annum
- 60 days: 1.25% per annum
- 90 days: 1.50% per annum
- 180 days: 1.75% per annum
- 360 days: 2.00% per annum

Interest may be credited to a linked savings account monthly, quarterly, or at maturity, depending on the placement agreement.

## Pre-termination

Pre-termination before maturity is allowed but is subject to a pre-termination penalty. The interest rate applied will be reduced and a processing fee may apply. Please consult your branch for the specific pre-termination terms.

## Automatic Rollover

Time deposits can be set to automatically roll over at maturity at the prevailing rate at that time, unless you instruct otherwise.

## Online and Mobile Banking

Our digital banking platform is available through the mobile app (iOS and Android) and the online banking portal on our official website.

## Key Features

- **Account overview** — View balances and recent transactions for all linked accounts
- **Fund transfers** — Transfer to accounts within the bank and to other banks via InstaPay and PESONet
- **Bill payments** — Pay utility bills, credit cards, loans, and government contributions
- **Card management** — Block, unblock, or set spending limits on your debit or credit card
- **Transaction history** — View and download transaction history for up to 12 months
- **QR code payments** — Scan and pay at participating merchants

## Security Features

- Two-factor authentication (2FA) via one-time password (OTP) sent to your registered mobile number
- Biometric login (fingerprint or face recognition) on supported devices
- Automatic session timeout after a period of inactivity

## Registration

To register for online banking, visit any branch and request activation, or download the mobile app and follow the self-registration process using your existing account details and a valid mobile number.

## Transaction Limits

Default daily transaction limits apply. You may request a limit increase through the branch or through the mobile app, subject to verification.

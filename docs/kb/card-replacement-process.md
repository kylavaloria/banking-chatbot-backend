## How to Request a Replacement Card

You may request a replacement card if your card is expired, damaged, or if you wish to upgrade to a new card design.

### Replacement Due to Expiry

Cards are automatically reissued before the expiry date. You will receive your new card by mail or you may pick it up at your registered branch, depending on your account setup.

### Replacement Due to Damage

To replace a damaged card:
1. Visit your nearest branch and bring a valid ID
2. Surrender the damaged card
3. Complete a card replacement request form
4. Pay the card replacement fee of PHP 250

The replacement card is typically ready within 3 to 5 banking days.

### Replacement Due to Loss or Theft

If your card has been lost or stolen, report it immediately to block the card and prevent unauthorized use. See the Lost Card Reporting document for the full procedure. After blocking, a replacement card will be issued upon your request.

### Card Delivery

Replacement cards can be delivered to your registered mailing address or picked up at the branch. In-branch pickup is typically faster.

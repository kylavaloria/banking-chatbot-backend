## Fund Transfer Processing Times

### Transfers Within the Same Bank

Transfers between accounts within our bank are processed immediately and are available to the recipient in real time, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

### Transfers to Other Banks (InstaPay)

InstaPay transfers to other participating banks are processed in real time during the service window. Most transfers are received by the beneficiary within a few minutes. The daily transaction limit for InstaPay is PHP 50,000.

### Transfers to Other Banks (PESONet)

PESONet transfers are processed in batch. Transfers submitted before the cut-off time of 3:00 PM on a banking day are credited to the beneficiary on the same day. Transfers submitted after the cut-off or on non-banking days are processed on the next banking day.

### International Wire Transfers

International wire transfers typically take 3 to 5 banking days to reach the beneficiary, depending on the destination country and the correspondent banks involved. Additional bank charges from intermediary banks may apply.

## What To Do if a Transfer Has Not Arrived

If a transfer has not been received after the expected processing period, the sender should file a transfer inquiry through any of the following channels:
- Contact our 24/7 customer service hotline
- Visit the nearest branch with the transaction reference number
- Submit an inquiry through the mobile banking application

Our team will investigate and provide a resolution within 5 to 7 banking days.

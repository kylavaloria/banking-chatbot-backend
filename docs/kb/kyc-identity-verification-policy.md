## KYC (Know Your Customer) and Identity Verification

As part of our regulatory obligations under the Anti-Money Laundering Act (AMLA) and Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (BSP) regulations, we are required to verify and periodically update the identity of all account holders.

## When KYC is Required

KYC verification is required in the following situations:
- Opening a new account
- Conducting transactions above certain thresholds
- Annual or periodic account review
- When the bank flags a change in account activity patterns

## What You Need to Provide

- Primary government-issued ID with photo and signature
- Updated contact information (mobile number and email address)
- Proof of address (if your ID does not reflect your current address)
- Source of funds declaration (for certain account types or transaction sizes)

## How to Complete a KYC Update

You may update your KYC information:
- At any branch — bring your valid ID and completed KYC update form
- Through the mobile banking app — limited updates may be done digitally depending on your account type

## Consequences of Non-Compliance

Accounts that have not completed required KYC updates within the given deadline may be subject to transaction restrictions until the update is completed. We will notify you in advance of any required updates through your registered contact details.

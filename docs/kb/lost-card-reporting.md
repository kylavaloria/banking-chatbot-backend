## How to Report a Lost or Stolen Card

If your card has been lost or stolen, act immediately to prevent unauthorized transactions.

## Step 1: Block Your Card Immediately

You can block your card through any of these channels:
- **Mobile banking app** — Go to Card Management and select "Block Card"
- **24/7 customer service hotline** — Call and request an immediate card block
- **SMS banking** — Send BLOCK [last 4 digits of card] to our SMS banking number

The block takes effect immediately once confirmed.

## Step 2: Report to the Bank

After blocking the card, file an official lost/stolen card report:
- Call our customer service hotline and ask for a lost card report reference number
- Or visit the nearest branch with a valid ID

## Step 3: Review Recent Transactions

Check your recent transaction history through the mobile app or online banking and report any unauthorized transactions immediately. File a dispute for any charges you do not recognize.

## Step 4: Request a Replacement Card

Once the card is blocked and the report is filed, you may request a replacement card. See the Card Replacement Process document for details.

## Important Notes

- Temporary blocks can be lifted if you find your card. Permanent blocks cannot be reversed and will require a replacement card.
- Report the loss to local authorities if theft is suspected — a police report may be required for some dispute investigations.
